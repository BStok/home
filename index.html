the pipeline
01
Clone
Pulls your repo from the URL you give it. Public repos, no setup.
02
Detect
Reads your files to figure out the framework, port, and start command.
03
Build
Generates the right Dockerfile and builds a container image.
04
Deploy
Runs your container and routes traffic to it through HTTPS.
05
Watch
Push to main and Parsley rebuilds and redeploys automatically.
